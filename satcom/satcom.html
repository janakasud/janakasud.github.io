About

Welcome to the Satellite Communication (Satcom) section of Janaka's Journal. Here, you'll find articles and resources dedicated to the fascinating world of satellite communications, including link budget fundamentals, system design, and emerging technologies in the field.

Whether you're a student, professional, or enthusiast, this section aims to provide valuable insights and knowledge to help you understand and navigate the complexities of satellite communication systems.
